Contributing to New Jersey Science Olympiad

Give directly to New Jersey Science Olympiad! 100% of your donation is tax deductible and will go to fund regional and State Science Olympiad tournaments for New Jersey elementary, middle and high school students. Please consider making a donation today!

The New Jersey Science Olympiad is a non-profit, tax-exempt 501(C)(3) organization designed to improve the quality of science education and to prepare the scientific and technological leaders of tomorrow. The Science Olympiad has received national acclaim from business and industry, government and education. Many companies match tax-deductible gifts. Please check with your company's matching gifts' coordinator for details. Support includes such things as helping with judging a tournament or funding for medals and trophies, facilities, programs and event expenses. Contributions of $500 to $5000 or more from corporations and foundations to the NJ State Science Olympiad organization have a significant affect on the quality and scope of the experiences that we are able to offer each year.

NJ Science Olympiad is a 501-C-3 tax-deductible charity. EIN #86-1106839
