2020 New Jersey Trial Events

Trial Events to Count Toward Team Placement in 2020

The NJSO Steering Committee has voted to include scores earned in trial events in calculating a teamâ€™s total score at New Jersey tournaments. Team alternates may compete only in trial events. If a team does not have alternates or a sufficient number of alternates, then regular team members, or a combination of regular team members and alternates, may compete in the trial events.
Rationale: To validate the time and effort expended both by team alternates and by event officials in preparing for the trial event(s).

Trial events are open to all regular team members as well as team alternates. Alternates may be additional Grade 9 students for Division B and additional grade 12 students for Division C which exceed the rules for the regular team.

Trial Events (and rules) will be posted when available
